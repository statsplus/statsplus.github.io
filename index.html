About STATS+ (StatsPlus)

STATS+ (also known as StatsPlus) is a fan-made mod that enhances and extends in-game statistics. This wiki documents its features, setup, and community information.

This project is not official and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by StatsPlus LLC. All names are used purely to describe this fan-made modification.
